Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Introduction

XSIM Cambodia ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Phone Number: We collect your phone number solely for the purpose of SIM-based authentication. Your phone number is used to identify your device and send one-time verification codes.

Push Notification Token: We collect your device's push notification token to deliver verification codes via push notifications.

No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, locations, browsing history, or any other personal information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To register your device for SIM-based authentication
  • To send one-time verification codes via push notifications
  • To verify your identity on government web portals

Data Storage

Your phone number and push notification token are stored securely on Cloudflare's global network with encryption at rest. Data is automatically deleted after 30 days of inactivity.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — for delivering push notifications
  • Cloudflare Workers — for secure server-side processing

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to provide our authentication service.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure token storage, and one-time-use verification codes.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
